This directory contains files (in bed format,=: [chr, start, end, name]) identifying
regions of high linkage disequilibrium (LD) that should be excluded from PCA:

- flashpca_exclusion_regions_hg19.txt
	- source: https://github.com/gabraham/flashpca
	- date downloaded : 08/12/2022
	
- high-LD-regions-hg19-GRCh37.txt , high-LD-regions-hg38-GRCh38.txt
	- source: https://github.com/cran/plinkQC/tree/master/inst/extdata and https://genome.sph.umich.edu/wiki/Regions_of_high_linkage_disequilibrium_(LD)#cite_note-3
	- date: 08/12/2022
